Welcome to the About Page

The motive of this webapp is to spread awareness about diagnosis of Diabetes at early stage so that it can be cured easily . Diabetes mellitues is a syndrome characterized by metabolic disorder and abnormal rise in the concentration of Blood sugar caused due to insulin deficiency , the reason for this is unhealthy life style and history of relatives and the genetic relationship of those relatives to the subject. So for early diagnosis of it to reduce the required cost and the valuable time of people , the proposed system is effective with an accuracy of 80% and the dataset used is Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset. The webapp will have more features in upcomming time so stay tune me.
